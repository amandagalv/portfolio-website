About

Hi, I’m Amanda. I’m a UX Designer with a background in Architecture. I’ve been designing for more about 10 years. I love the process, the problem-solving, the creative side of architecture but I’ve always been fascinated by how impactful the digital universe can be on our behaviour as a society. UX put all my passions together so it was a natural choice to migrate. I love working in a data-driven environment, to understand users and to apply that sense of user flow and aesthetics that I’ve learned from my background into an intuitive digital experience.
